Understanding Common Health Risks

Knowledge is the first step toward prevention

Heart Disease

Heart disease remains the leading cause of death worldwide. Risk factors include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking, and family history.

Early detection can significantly improve outcomes and quality of life.

Heart Attack

Heart attacks occur when blood flow to part of the heart is blocked. Recognizing early warning signs can be life-saving.

Our prediction model analyzes multiple factors to assess your risk level.

Diabetes

Diabetes affects how your body turns food into energy. Early detection and lifestyle modifications can prevent or delay complications.

Our system helps identify pre-diabetic conditions before they progress.
